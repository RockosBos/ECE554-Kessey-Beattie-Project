Exp4_Starter: Displays time and temperature every second in the terminal. Pressing SW1 makes the LED flash red or blue.

gpio_Tivaware: Flashes a green LED every 0.5 seconds or so.

led-sw-int_Final: Turns green LED on or off when SW1 is pressed.

serial-comm: When 'r' is typed, '*' is returned in the terminal window. When 'g' is typed, '-' is returned in the terminal window.
